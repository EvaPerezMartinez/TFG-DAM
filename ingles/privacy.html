Privacy Policy

At Paylum, we are committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of our users. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information in connection with the use of our application and website.

1. Data Controller

The company responsible for data processing is Paylum, which develops multi-platform accounting and financial management solutions.

You can contact us using the contact form available in the application or on the official website.

2. Information We Collect

When using the application or interacting with the website, we may collect the following personal data:

  • - Full names
  • - Email address
  • - Billing and accounting information entered by the user in the application
  • - Technical data such as IP address, device type, operating system, and usage statistics (to improve the user experience)

3. Purpose of Processing

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • - Enable the creation and management of user accounts
  • - Facilitate the use of the application's functionalities (invoicing, accounting, and financial management)
  • - Respond to enquiries made via the contact form
  • - Send service-related communications (security notifications, application updates)
  • - Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Data Retention

The data will be kept for the duration of the contractual relationship with the user and, subsequently, for the time necessary to comply with the corresponding legal obligations.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • - Access your personal data
  • - Rectify inaccurate data
  • - Request the deletion of data when it is no longer necessary
  • - Limit or oppose the processing of your data
  • - Request the portability of your data to another provider

To exercise these rights, users may contact us via the contact form or support email address.

6. Data Security

At Paylum, we use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure or destruction.

In addition, the application integrates database encryption using technologies such as SQLCipher to ensure the security of accounting information.

7. Use in Desktop and Mobile Applications

Our application is available on Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS, and all versions offer the same privacy and data protection guarantees. Data stored in the local database is encrypted for added security.

8. Cookies and Web Browsing

The Paylum website uses technical and analytical cookies to improve the browsing experience. Users can configure or reject the use of cookies at any time from their browser.

9. Policy Update

Paylum may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any relevant changes via notices in the application or on the website.
